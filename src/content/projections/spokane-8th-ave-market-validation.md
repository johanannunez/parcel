## Key Assumptions

- **Nightly rate ($145):** Between Spokane median ($130) and top 25% ($180). Defensible for a professionally managed, well furnished 2BR.
- **Management fee (20%):** Lower end of full service range (15–40%). Honest for a property management company offering transparent pricing.
- **Cleaning fee ($200/stay):** High end of the 2BR market ($72–$145 average). Consider lowering to $150 and rolling $15–$20 into the nightly rate for better Airbnb conversion.
- **Cleaner payout ($130):** Middle of 2BR range. Standard.
- **3 night average stay:** Reasonable for Spokane's market mix.

## Notes for Owner Conversations

1. **Lead with the moderate scenario** as the "expected" outcome. Show conservative as the floor and top performer as the ceiling.
2. **Year 1 ramp up:** Expect 10–20% below steady state while reviews accumulate. First 3 months are typically the slowest.
3. **Occupancy is the swing factor.** The nightly rate is market competitive; the difference between scenarios is almost entirely how many nights are booked.
4. **Cleaning fee strategy:** Airbnb's search algorithm factors total trip cost. A $200 cleaning fee on a $145/night, 2 night stay adds 69% to the room cost. Lowering it (or bundling into the rate) can improve booking conversion and therefore occupancy.
5. **These numbers do not include:** utilities, insurance, property taxes, HOA, mortgage payments, or furnishing costs. These are operating income projections only.
